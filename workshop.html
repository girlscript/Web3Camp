Remote WorkShops

A remote workshop is a workshop conducted online, where participants, speakers, and organizers are in different physical locations.

Guideline for Organisers are as follows :

Before the workshop

1. Determine the number of days/hours needed for the workshop

2. Decide on dates and times

3. Providing the name of the workshop, dates, expected participants to the Girlscript team.

4. Maintaining a report and providing registration details of the participants to the Girlscript team

5. Promote/advertise the workshop

Contact Speakers

1. Discuss their availability

2. Invite instructors to come a few minutes before the event to test the equipment available for the workshop.

In Person WorkShops

An in-person workshop is any workshop where participants, speakers, and organizers, all meet in the same physical location. Participants can work together in teams and support from coaches is done face-to-face.

These workshops will be conducted only when the conditions are safe and under covid-19 guidelines

Guideline for Organisers are as follows :

Before the workshop

1. Determine the number of days/hours needed for the workshop

2. Decide on dates, times, and venue of the workshop

3. Providing the name of the workshop, dates, expected participants, and venue details to the Girlscript team.

4. Set up the training or meeting venue ahead of time with labeled seating areas.

5. Select a venue with numerous windows that can be opened to ensure good airflow. Avoid venues with few windows.

6. Plan for equipment and services (computer, screen, microphone, etc.)

7. Maintaining a report and providing registration details of the participants to the Girlscript team

8. Promote/advertise the workshop

During the Workshop

1. Provide guidelines/requirements to participants in advance of training or meetings so they know what to expect.

2. Make sure chairs or benches should be spaced at least 2 meters apart.

3. Ask attendees to commit to a safety code of conduct.

4. Encourage frequent use of the hygiene stations, including upon entry into the training or meeting venue.

5. Keep a list of questions that need to be followed up after the workshop.

6. Provide workshop evaluations to participants to complete after the end of the workshop

7. Distribute certificates to attendees

During the workshop

1. Discuss their availability

2. Assist instructors with setting up the classroom

3. Arrange travel and lodging for the instructors (expenses provide by team Girlscript)

4. Organize food service, refreshments(expenses provide by team Girlscript)
