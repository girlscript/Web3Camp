REMOTE WORKSHOP
IN-PERSON WORKSHOP
A remote workshop is a workshop conducted online, where participants, speakers, and organizers are in different physical locations.

GUIDELINES

Before the Workshop

  • Determine the number of days/hours needed for the workshop
  • Decide on dates and times
  • Providing the name of the workshop, dates, expected participants to the Girlscript team.
  • Maintaining a report and providing registration details of the participants to the Girlscript team
  • Promote/advertise the workshop

Contact Speakers

  • Discuss their availability
  • Invite instructors to come a few minutes before the event to test the equipment available for the workshop.

During the Workshop

  • Make sure technical support is available to contact during the workshop.
  • Keep a list of questions that need to be followed up after the workshop.
  • Provide workshop evaluations to participants to complete after the end of the workshop
  • Distribute certificates to attendees
An in-person workshop is any workshop where participants, speakers, and organizers, all meet in the same physical location. Participants can work together in teams and support from coaches is done face-to-face.
These workshops will be conducted only when the conditions are safe and under covid-19 guidelines

GUIDELINES

Before the workshop

  • Provide guidelines/requirements to participants in advance of training or meetings so they know what to expect.
  • Make sure chairs or benches should be spaced at least 2 meters apart.
  • Ask attendees to commit to a safety code of conduct.
  • Encourage frequent use of the hygiene stations, including upon entry into the training or meeting venue.
  • Keep a list of questions that need to be followed up after the workshop.
  • Provide workshop evaluations to participants to complete after the end of the workshop.
  • Distribute certificates to attendees

Contact Speakers

  • Discuss their availability
  • Invite instructors to come a few minutes before the event to test the equipment available for the workshop.

During the Workshop

  • Discuss their availability
  • Assist instructors with setting up the classroom
  • Arrange travel and lodging for the instructors (expenses provide by team Girlscript)
  • Organize food service, refreshments(expenses provide by team Girlscript)
